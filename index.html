A Sanctuary in the Heart of Bellevue

Nestled in Bellevue's most desirable Meydenbauer neighborhood, this stunning custom home combines timeless luxury with the warmth of a true family retreat. Minutes from downtown Bellevue, yet tucked away on a quiet, private low-traffic street — a rare sense of peace and security.
